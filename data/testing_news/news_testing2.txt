Coca-Cola Declares $0.47 Quarterly Dividend Per Share

Atlanta, GA – February 3, 2025 – The Coca-Cola Company (NYSE: KO) has announced the approval of a quarterly cash dividend of $0.47 per common share, remaining in line with the previous quarter.

The dividend will be payable on April 1, 2025, to shareholders of record as of March 15, 2025, with an ex-dividend date of March 14, 2025.

Dividend Details:

Amount: $0.47 per share

Annual Yield: 3.15%

Payment Date: April 1, 2025

Ex-Dividend Date: March 14, 2025

With this announcement, Coca-Cola continues its 62-year streak of consecutive dividend increases, solidifying its position as one of the most reliable income-generating stocks for investors.

The beverage giant has seen an 8% rise in net revenue over the last quarter, driven by strong demand in sugar-free and energy drink segments, as well as expansion in emerging markets.

For more information on Coca-Cola’s financial performance, visit Coca-Cola Investor Relations.
