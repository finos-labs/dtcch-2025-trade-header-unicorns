Unilever Announces Dividend

The business recently disclosed a quarterly dividend, which will be paid on Wednesday, April 10th. Stockholders of record on Wednesday, March 20th will be given a dividend of £0.95 per share. The ex-dividend date of this dividend is Wednesday, March 20th. This represents a £3.80 dividend on an annualized basis and a dividend yield of 4.12%. Unilever’s dividend payout ratio (DPR) is 68.75%.
