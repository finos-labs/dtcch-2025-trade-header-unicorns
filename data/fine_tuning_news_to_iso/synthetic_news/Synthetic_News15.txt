Walmart Inc Announces Semi-Annual Dividend

New York, USA – February 4, 2025 – Walmart Inc (WMT) declared a semi-annual dividend of €0.75 per share, maintaining its consistent payout policy. The dividend is payable on April 20, 2025, to shareholders who are on record as of April 5, 2025, with an ex-dividend date of April 4, 2025. The company continues to demonstrate stable financial performance across its markets.
