MedHealth Corp. Increases Annual Dividend Amid Growth

Zurich, Switzerland – February 4, 2025 – MedHealth Corp. (SWX: MHC) announced an annual dividend of CHF 2.00 per share, up from CHF 1.80 the previous year, reflecting a 11% increase. The dividend will be paid on May 15, 2025, to shareholders recorded by April 30, 2025, with an ex-dividend date of April 29, 2025. The company's strong earnings growth in the healthcare sector has facilitated this enhanced return to shareholders.
